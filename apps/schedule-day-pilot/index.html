Privacy Policy — Day Pilot (hishab_planner)

Last updated: 2026-04-25

Day Pilot (“the app”) helps you manage personal schedules and finance records. This policy explains what data the app processes, why, and your choices.

Summary

  • Most data stays on your device.
  • Optional Google Drive backup is only used if you connect your Google account and run Backup/Auto-backup.
  • We do not sell your personal data.

Data the app stores/processes

Data you enter

  • Schedule events (title, description, date/time, location, reminder settings).
  • Finance records (expenses, lending/borrowing, collections, contacts you add inside the app).
  • Optional images you attach (e.g., avatars/receipts), if you choose to pick or capture images.

Device/app data

  • Notification scheduling data (local notification ids and schedules).
  • App settings (language, theme, reminder defaults, alarm tone, security settings).

Google account (optional)

If you connect Google Drive for backups, the app uses Google Sign-In to access your Google account email address (to show which account is connected) and to obtain authorization to upload/download backups.

How your data is used

  • To provide core features: showing your schedules/records, calculating totals, and sending reminders.
  • To back up and restore your app data (only when you connect Google Drive and use backup/restore).

Backups to Google Drive (optional)

  • If enabled, backups are uploaded to your Google Drive appDataFolder (an app-private area not visible in your normal Drive files list).
  • Backups may include your entered data and any images you chose to store in the app (when image backup is enabled).
  • You can disconnect Google Drive at any time. You can also delete backups from inside the app’s Backup screen.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. The app does not share your data with third parties except:

  • Google services required for sign-in and Drive backup (Google Sign-In, Google Drive API).

Permissions (Android)

The app may request these permissions depending on your use:

  • Notifications / alarms: to show reminders and (optionally) ring alarm-style alerts.
  • Internet/network state: for Google Drive backup/restore.
  • Biometric unlock: if you enable fingerprint unlock.
  • Photos/files/camera access: only when you choose to pick/import images or files.

Security

The app supports optional PIN and biometric unlock. No method of storage or transmission is 100% secure, but we aim to use reasonable safeguards.

Data retention & deletion

  • Your data remains on your device until you delete it (e.g., “Clear all data” if available) or uninstall the app.
  • If you enabled Google Drive backups, you can delete backups from the app’s Backup screen or by disconnecting and removing app data from your Google account.

Children’s privacy

The app is not intended for children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date will reflect the latest changes.

Questions or Concerns?
